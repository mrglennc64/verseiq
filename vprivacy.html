Privacy Policy – VerseIQ

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

VerseIQ is a music intelligence tool that analyzes publicly available Spotify metadata to help artists, labels, and rights holders identify international gaps, opportunities, and metadata inconsistencies. We operate independently and are committed to transparency and data minimalism.

2. Data we access

VerseIQ uses the Spotify Web API to access public metadata only: artists, tracks, albums, playlists, genres, and popularity metrics. We do not access private listening history, private playlists, or any user-specific streaming data. No personal identifiers are collected unless explicitly provided via contact forms.

✓ Only public Spotify metadata
✓ No private listening activity
✓ No user profile information
✓ No third-party tracking or advertising

3. How we use the data

We use metadata to generate analytics and insights — including territory gap analysis, rights registration patterns, and catalog performance indicators. Data is used solely to improve music rights visibility and workflow efficiency. We do not sell data or use it for advertising, retargeting, or any form of behavioral marketing.

4. Data retention

We minimize storage and retain data only as long as needed to provide the service or to fulfill analytical requests. Metadata aggregates are anonymized where possible. You may request deletion of any stored reference data at any time by contacting us directly. Cache and logs are automatically rotated every 30 days.

5. Your rights & control

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete information related to your use of VerseIQ. Since we do not collect personal data via Spotify OAuth (only public metadata), your privacy remains protected by design. For any inquiry or deletion request, email us using the contact below.
