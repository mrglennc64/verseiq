You're not selling a tool. You're selling **financial recovery**.

**The Problem:** Musicians are losing 10-30% of their royalties because they're not registered with SoundExchange (or registered incorrectly). Every unregistered track = thousands of dollars in missed income annually.

**Your Solution:** VerseIQ identifies what's missing and helps recover it.

**The Business:** $X – $X million TAM (total addressable market) of unrecovered royalties across independent artists globally.

## Revenue Model Analysis

### Model A: SaaS Subscription (€19–49/month)

**Pros:**
- Predictable recurring revenue
- Simple billing
- Clear pricing

**Cons:**
- Low LTV (lifetime value) for small artists
- Churn risk if user gets bored after analysis
- Doesn't align with "help people recover money"

**Realistic Metrics:**
- 1,000 free users → 50 STARTER (5% conversion)
- 50 STARTER → 20 PRO (40% upgrade)
- LTV = €49 × 12 months × 2 years = €1,176
- CAC (customer acquisition cost) = ~€20
- LTV/CAC ratio = 59x (excellent)

### Model B: Revenue Share (10–20%)

**Pros:**
- Aligns incentives (we make money when they make money)
- No resistance from price-sensitive artists
- Lower churn (we're a revenue partner, not a cost)
- Trust builder

**Cons:**
- Variable revenue (hard to forecast)
- Requires more operational overhead
- Requires trust + transparency

**Realistic Metrics:**
- 50 artists × €5,000/year recovered × 15% = €37,500/year
- After 2 years: 200+ artists × €37,500 = €1.5M revenue/year

### Model C: Hybrid (Recommended) ✅

**€19 STARTER (subscription)** + **15–20% revenue share option for PRO/ENTERPRISE**

This gives users a choice:
- DIY path: Pay subscription, register yourself
- Hands-off path: Revenue share (we manage recovery)
